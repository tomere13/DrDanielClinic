## What Each Feature Does

### 1. **Text Size** ğŸ“

**What it does:**

- Makes all text on the website bigger or smaller
- Helps people with visual impairments read more easily

**Options:**

- **1x** (Normal) - Default size (16px)
- **1.25x** (Large) - 25% bigger (20px)
- **1.5x** (Extra Large) - 50% bigger (24px)

---

### 2. **Contrast Mode** ğŸ¨

**What it does:**

- Changes colors to high contrast (easier to read)
- Removes background images and shadows
- Helps people with visual impairments or color blindness

**Options:**

- **Normal Contrast** - Regular colorful design
- **Yellow on Black** - Yellow text on pure black background
- **White on Black** - White text on pure black background

**How to use:**

1. Click accessibility button
2. Under "Contrast Mode", choose an option
3. Entire website changes to high contrast

**Good for:**

- Low vision
- Color blindness
- Light sensitivity
- Bright environments

---

### 3. **Reduce Motion** ğŸ¬

**What it does:**

- Stops all animations and transitions
- Makes website static (no floating, fading, or moving elements)
- Prevents motion sickness or dizziness

**How to use:**

1. Click accessibility button
2. Under "Motion Preference", click "Reduce Motion"
3. Button turns blue when ON
4. Click again to turn OFF

**What changes:**

- No typewriter effect
- No floating shapes
- No fade-in animations
- No hover effects
- Instant page transitions

**Good for:**

- Vestibular disorders
- Motion sensitivity
- Epilepsy concerns
- ADHD (less distraction)

---

### 4. **Keyboard Navigation (Focus Indicators)** âŒ¨ï¸

**What it does:**

- Shows a **blue outline** around clickable items when you press Tab
- Lets you navigate the website without a mouse
- **ALWAYS ON** - you don't need to enable it

**How to use:**

1. Press **Tab** key on your keyboard
2. See blue outline move between buttons, links, and form fields
3. Press **Enter** or **Space** to click
4. Press **Shift + Tab** to go backwards

**What you'll see:**

- Thick blue outline (4px)
- Glowing shadow around focused element
- Moves as you press Tab

**Good for:**

- Motor disabilities
- Blind users with screen readers
- Power users who prefer keyboard
- Anyone who doesn't use a mouse

## ğŸ” Troubleshooting

### "I don't see the accessibility button"

**Look for:**

- **Blue circle** with accessibility icon (â™¿)
- **Bottom-left corner** of screen
- Floats above content

**If you still don't see it:**

- Scroll up or down (it should stay visible)
- Refresh the page
- Check if browser extensions are blocking it

### "High Contrast isn't working"

**Check:**

1. Did you click one of the high contrast options?
2. Look for blue checkmark on selected option
3. Refresh the page if needed
4. Try both Yellow and White options

### "Reduce Motion isn't working"

**The animations that stop:**

- Hero typewriter effect
- Floating background shapes
- Card hover animations
- Fade-in effects

**What still works:**

- All navigation
- All buttons
- Contact form
- Everything functions normally (just no motion)

### "Keyboard navigation (Tab) doesn't work"

**Make sure:**

- You're pressing Tab (not Enter)
- You see the blue outline
- Try on clickable elements (buttons, links)

**On Mac:**

- May need to enable: System Preferences â†’ Keyboard â†’ Shortcuts â†’ "Use keyboard navigation to move focus between controls"
